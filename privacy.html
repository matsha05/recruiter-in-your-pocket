Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

TL;DR: We only collect what we need to make the product work. Your resume data is processed for feedback but not stored long-term. We don't sell your info.

What We Collect

When you use Recruiter in Your Pocket, we may collect:

  • Resume text: The content you paste or upload. This is processed by our AI to generate feedback and is not permanently stored unless you create an account.
  • Job descriptions: If you provide one for alignment analysis, it's processed along with your resume.
  • Email address: If you sign in, we collect your email to manage your account and purchased passes.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics like page views and feature usage to improve the product (no resume content is included).
  • Payment info: Handled securely by Stripe. We don't store your credit card details.

How We Use Your Data

  • To generate AI-powered resume feedback
  • To manage your account and purchased passes
  • To send transactional emails (login codes, receipts)
  • To improve the product (aggregated, anonymized usage data only)

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your personal information
  • We don't use your resume content to train AI models
  • We don't share your data with third parties for marketing
  • We don't store resume text longer than necessary for processing

Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Track your free report usage
  • Remember your preferences (like dark mode)

We may use analytics cookies (Mixpanel) to understand usage patterns. These do not contain your resume content.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • OpenAI: Powers our AI feedback. Resume text is sent to OpenAI's API for processing. See their privacy policy.
  • Stripe: Handles payments securely. See their privacy policy.
  • Vercel: Hosts our application. See their privacy policy.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication, and rate limiting to protect your data.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our website.
