How we score

We evaluate your resume on how clearly your story lands to a recruiter in a first read. The score reflects:

  • Impact & Outcomes — Are results visible and specific?
  • Clarity & Structure — Is it easy to scan and understand?
  • Story & Narrative — Does your career arc make sense?
  • Readability — Can a recruiter get your value in seconds?

This is calibrated against real resumes reviewed by experienced recruiters. 80+ is genuinely strong for most roles.
