## Core Workflow Summary

1. Clip jobs from boards via Chrome extension
2. Organize applications in Kanban-style dashboard
3. Build or tailor resumes with AI assistance
4. Track contacts, interviews, and follow-ups
5. Generate cover letters matched to job descriptions

## Feature Highlights

- **Job Tracker Dashboard**: Visual Kanban board for application status
- **Chrome Extension**: One-click job clipping from LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.
- **Map View**: Visualize job proximity to home location
- **AI Resume Builder**: Templates with AI-generated content
- **Resume Tailor**: Keyword extraction and matching from JDs
- **Resume Checker**: Scoring based on content and completion
- **Cover Letter Generator**: AI-drafted letters matched to jobs
- **Application Autofill**: Speed up form completion
- **Contact CRM**: Manage professional networking contacts

## Notable Strengths

1. **Visual organization**: Kanban board reduces application chaos
2. **Map view**: Unique feature for location-aware job seekers
3. **Chrome extension**: Seamless job clipping workflow
4. **Contact CRM**: Integrates networking into job search
5. **Free tier value**: 100 jobs is generous for testing
6. **Autofill feature**: Reduces repetitive form work

## Notable Weaknesses

1. **Premium pricing** at $40/mo (high end of market)
2. **Tracking-first focus** may overwhelm pure resume seekers
3. **Resume review depth** less than dedicated review tools
4. **No LinkedIn optimization** feature
5. **Complexity** for users who just want resume help
