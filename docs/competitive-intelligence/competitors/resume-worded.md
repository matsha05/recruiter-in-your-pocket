# Resume Worded

**Last verified date:** 2025-12-27

## Overview

| Field | Value |
|:------|:------|
| **Product** | Resume Worded |
| **URL** | https://resumeworded.com |
| **Category** | AI Resume Feedback / LinkedIn Optimization |
| **Target user** | Professionals seeking expert-level feedback on resume and LinkedIn profile |

## Positioning Promise

"Get more interviews with our AI-powered resume and LinkedIn optimizer" — Resume Worded positions as a dual-track coach that provides actionable feedback on both resume documents and LinkedIn profiles.

## Core Workflow Summary

1. Upload resume PDF or paste LinkedIn URL
2. AI analyzes against "20 checks" for quality
3. User receives overall score + section-by-section feedback
4. Iterate based on suggestions
5. Optional LinkedIn profile optimization flow

## Feature Highlights

- **Resume Score**: Overall quality rating based on 20 criteria
- **LinkedIn Review**: Profile SEO and keyword optimization
- **Detailed Feedback**: Section-by-section improvement suggestions
- **Keyword Identification**: Target keyword recommendations
- **250+ Resume Lines Library**: Real example improvements
- **Professional Templates**: ATS-friendly designs
- **Privacy Reassurance**: Explicit data protection messaging on upload

## Pricing and Packaging

| Plan | Price | Notes |
|:-----|:------|:------|
| **Free** | $0 | Basic resume scoring, limited feedback |
| **Monthly** | $49/mo | Full access to all features |

**Conversion mechanics:**
- Free tier shows tantalizing score, gates detailed feedback
- Quarterly/annual discounts encourage commitment
- LinkedIn review drives second use case

## Output Quality and Trust Signals

- **Explicit privacy reassurance** on upload (standout trust signal)
- **Quantified "20 checks"** methodology feels rigorous
- **LinkedIn + Resume dual-track** increases perceived value
- **Dated UI** undermines premium perception

## Notable Strengths

1. **LinkedIn + Resume combo** captures both profile types
2. **Privacy messaging** builds trust at upload
3. **Section-specific feedback** is more actionable than single score
4. **Example library** provides concrete improvement patterns
5. **Established brand** in resume optimization category

## Notable Weaknesses

1. **Expensive** at $49/mo (premium tier competitors)
2. **Dated visual design** feels less premium than 2024 alternatives
3. **Generic AI tone** in feedback undermines expert credibility
4. **No Chrome extension** (missed retention opportunity)
5. **Limited workflow integration** — review only, not building

## Why Users Choose Them

- Dual LinkedIn + Resume optimization in one tool
- Established reputation and trust
- Section-specific feedback feels thorough
- Privacy reassurance addresses upload anxiety

## What We Should Steal

- **Privacy reassurance pattern**: Explicit "we don't sell data" on upload
- **Section-by-section feedback**: More actionable than single score
- **LinkedIn track**: Natural expansion for "Recruiter Studio"
- **Example library**: Real before/after improvements

## Where We Can Win

1. **Premium craft**: Modern editorial design vs dated UI
2. **Recruiter simulation**: "What they see" vs checklist optimization
3. **Price disruption**: $9 one-time vs $49/mo subscription
4. **Interactive rewrites**: "Red Pen" vs static suggestions
5. **Visual authority**: Fraunces typography vs generic SaaS
